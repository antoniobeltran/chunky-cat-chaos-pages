Privacy Policy

Rainbow Cat
Last updated: June 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Rainbow Cat ("the App") handles information. Rainbow Cat is a simple, kid-friendly game where players can fly a colorful cat around the screen and leave a rainbow trail behind. The App is designed to be enjoyed without collecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

The App does not require an account, login, or profile. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or any other personal information.

What the App Does Not Use

  • No advertising
  • No ad tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No social media integrations
  • No online accounts or cloud saves
  • No push notifications
  • No third-party data sharing

Information Processed on Your Device

All gameplay, including touch input, graphics, sound, and rainbow effects, happens entirely on your device. Any temporary data used while the game is running stays on your device and is never transmitted to us.

Apple App Store

If you download the App from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect information according to its own Privacy Policy. That information is collected and managed by Apple, not by us. For more information, visit Apple's Privacy Policy .

Children's Privacy

Rainbow Cat is designed to be family-friendly. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or adults. Because we do not collect personal information, no personal data is stored or shared by the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page along with the updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mingnobee@gmail.com or visit our Support page.
