Privacy Policy

Chunky Cat Chaos
Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chunky Cat Chaos (“the App”) handles information. The App is a paid mobile game. We designed it to work without collecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

The App does not require an account, login, or profile. We do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or other personal data while you use the App.

What the App Does Not Use

  • No advertising or ad tracking
  • No in-app analytics or usage tracking SDKs
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud save or online accounts
  • No push notifications
  • No third-party data sharing

Information Processed Only on Your Device

Gameplay (such as touch input, audio playback, and on-screen graphics) happens locally on your device. Any temporary data needed to run the game stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Apple App Store

If you purchase or download the App through the Apple App Store, Apple may collect information according to its own privacy policy. That processing is controlled by Apple, not by us. See Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Because we do not collect personal data, no parental consent is required for data collection by us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we change how data is handled, we will update this page and the “Last updated” date above.
