How to Play

  • Move: Touch and hold the screen, then drag away from the center.
  • Jump: While holding, drag upward.
  • Recharge: Stand still to refill your bar.
  • Orientation: The game is landscape-only. Rotate your phone sideways.

Common Questions

No sound?
Check your device volume and make sure the silent switch is off.

Game won’t open or crashes?
Try force-quitting the app, restarting your iPhone, and installing the latest iOS update. If it still fails, email us with your device details.
