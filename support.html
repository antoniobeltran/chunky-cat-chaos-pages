Rainbow Cat

Thanks for playing Rainbow Cat! If you have questions, found a bug, or have feedback, we'd love to hear from you.

mingnobee@gmail.com

When reporting a problem, please include your device model, iOS version, and a brief description of what happened. Screenshots or screen recordings are always helpful.

How to Play

  • Touch and drag to guide Rainbow Cat around the screen.
  • Fly freely and leave a colorful rainbow trail behind you.
  • There are no levels or timers—just relax, explore, and have fun!

Common Questions

No sound?
Make sure your device volume is turned up and your iPhone isn't in Silent Mode.

The game won't open or crashes?
Try closing the app completely, restarting your device, and installing the latest version of iOS. If the problem continues, please email us with your device details.

Refunds
Purchases made through the App Store are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

Privacy

Rainbow Cat does not collect personal information. Read our Privacy Policy to learn more.
